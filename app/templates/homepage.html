Purpose

This product uses text analysis technology to deeply mine data, extracting categories, classifications, entities, keywords, emotions, feelings, relationships, and grammar.

This product is dedicated to news sentiment analysis. Users can obtain positive, negative, neutral and other emotional information by uploading news texts, which has multiple meanings for individuals.

  • Information filtering
    Individuals can quickly obtain the emotional inclination of news, help judge the credibility and importance of information, and make more informed decisions.
  • Emotional regulation
    Understanding news emotions helps individuals manage their emotional reactions and avoid unnecessary anxiety and emotional fluctuations caused by negative news.
  • Decision basis
    In life or investment decisions, one can refer to the results of news sentiment analysis to help individuals assess risks and opportunities.

Benefits

  • Customer feedback insight:
    Analyzing customers' emotional responses to news reports helps businesses understand user needs and improve products and services.
  • Media content optimization:
    News organizations can adjust their reporting strategies and content based on audience emotions, thereby enhancing reader engagement and retention.
  • Social issue identification:
    By analyzing sentiment in news content, organizations can identify emerging social issues and take timely action through research or intervention.
  • Cross-cultural understanding:
    Sentiment analysis enables better recognition of culturally sensitive topics and helps institutions promote inclusivity and targeted communication across diverse audiences.
