Overview

This product uses text analysis technology to deeply mine data, extracting categories, classifications, entities, keywords, emotions, feelings, relationships, and grammar.

Natural Language Understanding (NLU) is a top-notch text analysis service that refers to the ability of computer systems to understand human language, involving extracting meaning from text, identifying intent, and comprehending context.

  • Intention recognition: Determine the user's intention, such as querying information, issuing instructions, etc.
  • Sentiment analysis: Determine the emotional orientation of the text, such as positive, negative, or neutral.
  • Entity Recognition: Extract specific information from text, such as names, locations, dates, etc.

  • Customer feedback insights: Analyzing customers' emotional reactions to news reports can help businesses understand user needs and optimize products and services.
  • Media content optimization: News organizations can adjust their reporting direction and content strategies based on audience emotional reactions to increase reader engagement.
  • Social problem identification: By analyzing news emotions, social concerns can be identified to assist relevant organizations in conducting research and intervention.
